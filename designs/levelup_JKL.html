Who We Are

We create digital tools and campaigns that interrupt cultural moments — like flying a plane banner over a football match to break the silence around rape in sports, pushing ITV to drop plastic surgery adverts from Love Island.

Working towards a world where people of all genders are loved and liberated from bodily and systemic violence. Through campaign coalitions and monthly workshops on bystander intervention.

About us

Who We Are

We create digital tools and campaigns that interrupt cultural moments to fight for gender justice — like flying a plane banner over a football match to break the silence around rape in sports, pushing ITV to drop plastic surgery adverts from Love Island and producing the UK's first media guidelines on reporting domestic abuse deaths.

Working towards a world where people of all genders are loved and liberated from bodily and systemic violence. Our movement includes feminists from a range of backgrounds and all genders, centering the voices and liberation of people who experience gender injustice.
